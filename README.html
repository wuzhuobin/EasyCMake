EasyCMake

This repository contains some useful CMakelists for fast making C++ projects.

It is quite easy to use. For people who don't know cmake while need to use cmake to make C++ project!

●SimpleTemplateCMakeLists
--Dependency with VTK & ITK & QT.
●vtk_itk_TemplateCMakeLists
--Dependency with VTK & ITK.
●ctk_TemplateCMakeLists
--Dependency with VTK & ITK & CTK & QT
●vmtk_TemplateCMakeLists
--Dependency with VTK & ITK & VMTK.
●LibDllCMakeLists
--Dependency with VTK & ITK & QT & specified windows dlls libs.
●ComplexTemplateCMakeLists
--Dependency with VTK & ITK & QT. And it is for building cmake package & install & exporting.
●LinguistTemplateCMakeLists
--Dependency with VTK & ITK & QT with option to support qtlinguist. Auto update *.ts and moving *.qm to folder 'bin'.
●OpenCV_TemplanteCMakeLists
--Dependency with OpenCV only.
●pcl_TemplateCMakeLists
--Dependency with pcl(Point Cloud Library) only.
●ResourcesTemplateCMakeLists
--Dependency with VTK & ITK & QT & specified resources. Resources can be copy to folder 'bin'.
